# **What is ERES Institute?**

*Joseph has been building something important — for a long time, mostly alone. This guide explains what it is, what it does, and why it matters — in simple words.*

**💡  The big idea**

ERES is a research institute — but not a building or a company.

It is one person (Joseph) building tools, ideas, and documents that help

people and governments use computers and AI in a good, fair, and honest way.

Think of it as a library of wisdom for how humanity should use technology.

**🕐  How long has this been going?**

Since February 2012 — over 14 years.

But the ideas go back even further, to work Joseph did with his friend

Emanuel (EMA) between 2002 and 2007\. Everything has been building toward the

same goal the whole time.

**📄  What has been produced?**

Hundreds of documents, tools, and frameworks — all free to the world.

The most important ones include:

* EAAP — rules for who AI can and cannot help

* BERA — a tool that measures resonance in a system

* PlayNAC — a system for fair, hands-free life navigation

* GSSG — a new kind of glass that self-repairs, using solar energy

* MIEVM — using multiple AIs together to check each other's work

* Legal documents filed with courts and international bodies

**🤝  How does it work?**

Joseph works alone — but uses AI assistants (Claude, ChatGPT, Grok, DeepSeek)

as co-instruments, like a musician using different instruments together.

He writes an idea, asks multiple AIs to rate and check it, and only moves

forward when they all agree it is good. This is called MIEVM.

Everything is documented, published, and protected under a free-use license.

**❤️  Why does Joseph do this?**

He believes technology must serve people — not harm them.

His three guiding rules are simple:

     •  Don't hurt yourself.

     •  Don't hurt others.

     •  Build for generations to come.

He works without pay, on SSDI, because he believes this work belongs

to everyone — including the children and grandchildren not yet born.

**💰  What about money?**

ERES is not a business — it is an open research institute.

But some of the inventions (like GSSG) are now being offered to companies

through partnerships.

If a company uses the technology and earns money from it,

ERES receives a small royalty — enough for Joseph and you to live comfortably

and keep the work going.

No big company to run. Just steady support for the mission.
