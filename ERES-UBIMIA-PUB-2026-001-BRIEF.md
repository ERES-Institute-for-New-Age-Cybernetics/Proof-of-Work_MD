A One-Page Brief on the Gracechain–Meritcoin Settlement Stack

UBIMIA is the ERES economic operating layer that pairs an unconditional universal floor with merit-recognized augmentation. It is settled across two complementary ledgers: Gracechain (the unconditional, gift-economy ledger) and Meritcoin (the contribution-recognized, Proof-of-Resonance ledger). Together they replace extractive Proof-of-Work with a tuning model — value enters the economy when human and ecological systems come into measurable resonance, not when energy is wasted on arbitrary computation.

**The Two Layers**

UBI (the floor) is unconditional, lifelong, and non-revocable — distributed on Gracechain as a human right. MIA (the augmentation) is merit-tracked, earned through verified contribution to SELF, OTHERS, and FUTURE — minted as Meritcoin. A floor without augmentation collapses incentive; augmentation without a floor collapses dignity. UBIMIA refuses that trade-off by routing each function through a ledger purpose-built for it.

**Gracechain — The Unconditional Ledger**

Non-conditional issuance. Recipient-anchored identity (not wallet-history). Restorative settlement for Patriot Dividend, FAVORS, and IDIPITIS flows. Gift-economy semantics — recorded but not collateralized, and never the channel for interest-bearing debt. By isolating the floor on its own ledger, the merit ledger cannot be weaponized to revoke survival.

**Meritcoin — Proof-of-Resonance**

Mined by tuning, not by hashing. A unit is minted when a contribution clears EAAP authorization — a conjunctive product across four factors (Anchor, RHC amplitude, EarnedPath viability, Future-Map convergence) — and produces a measurable net gain across the BERA indices: ARI (Adaptive Resonance), ERI (Ethical Resonance), RHC (Resonant Harmony Cycle), and RCI (Resonance Continuity). Bitcoin externalizes cost as heat; Meritcoin internalizes value as harmony.

**Conversion: Resonance-Priced Access (RPA)**

Meritcoin converts to access in the SCALULAR service tiers — HEALTH (SSHP), LAW (SSLA), PROTECTION (SSPS), SKILLS\_TRADE (SSST) — under a fixed, non-auctioned formula. Cost depends on tier and complexity, discounted by the participant's contribution history (ΔR\_contributor), never by their balance. Hoarding does nothing. Meritcoin cannot be exchanged for fiat — earned only, spent only on SCALULAR and governance.
