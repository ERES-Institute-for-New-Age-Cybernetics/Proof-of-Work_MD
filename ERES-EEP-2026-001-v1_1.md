# **1\. Preamble & Purpose**

This instrument formalizes the Enneagrammatic Signature as a recognized extension of the FAVORS trust stack (Fingerprint·Aura·Voice·ODOR·Retina·Signature) and establishes the architecture by which that signature can be used to connect a person in distress to real, accountable help — without requiring concealment, and without exposing the person to the institutional retribution that ordinarily keeps people silent.

The premise: a person’s behavioral signature inside a Community of Interest (COI) is already visible. Security is not achieved by hiding the pattern; it is achieved by aligning conduct with the pattern in a setting that will not punish the person for being seen. That alignment, made safely available, is itself a coping mechanism — for many, the difference between getting through the day and not.

# **2\. Canonical Grounding**

* Three Governing Principles — Don’t hurt yourself. Don’t hurt others. Build for generations to come.

* FAVORS — Signature is extended to include the Enneagrammatic behavioral pattern, alongside Fingerprint, Aura, Voice, ODOR, Retina.

* Proof-of-Resonance (PoR \= A×R×P×F) — security here is authenticity-as-coherence, not obscurity.

* SCALULAR fear-of-retribution elimination — no caller may be penalized, exposed, or institutionally retaliated against for the act of calling.

# **3\. Architecture Overview**

Three layers, independently functional, designed to operate together: L1 Access (the number), L2 Identity (the digital twin), L3 Escalation (exposure-as-liberation). See v1.0 §3 for the full failure-mode table; unchanged in v1.1.

# **4\. Layer 1 — Access (The Number)**

* Real and staffed by people trained to provide actual support — never an unattended ERES inbox.

* Reachable without precondition. No enrollment, no verification, no Enneagram type required to make first contact.

* Explicitly and permanently subordinate to licensed crisis infrastructure (Section 8). This layer routes; it does not replace.

# **5\. Layer 2 — Implementation Specification (NEW in v1.1)**

This section closes the Governance Utility gate identified by 3-of-4 MIEVM nodes (DeepSeek, Gemini, Claude) in the v1.0 review: the twin was described by function but not by build mechanism. The specification below is deliberately narrow — it is buildable, not aspirational.

## **5.1 Type Identification**

The caller self-administers a standard nine-type Enneagram instrument at the point of first voluntary disclosure. The type is never inferred, assigned, or suggested by the responder. If the caller does not know or does not wish to take the instrument, the twin remains null — a null twin is a valid, permanent, first-class state, not an incomplete one.

## **5.2 Twin Record Structure**

Where a twin exists, it holds exactly four fields: core type; optional wing; self-identified stress-direction behavior; self-identified growth-direction behavior. A fifth, free-text field (“what I reach for under pressure,” caller’s own words) is optional and caller-authored — never paraphrased or interpreted by the system.

## **5.3 Storage & Decoupling**

The twin is keyed to a caller-chosen passphrase or token, never to a name, phone number, or other real-world identifier. This decouples the pattern record from the caller’s legal identity, consistent with the privacy requirement named in v1.0 §6.

## **5.4 Revision & Deletion**

The caller may revise or delete the twin at any contact. Deletion is immediate and irreversible — no shadow copy, no retention window. This operationalizes “revocable independent of the sensor’s—or here, the system’s—physical presence,” carried over from the BERA-ODOR addendum’s consent language.

## **5.5 Responder Use**

**The twin is presented to the responder as context for tone, never as a script and never as a triage input. Responder training materials must state this distinction explicitly: the twin informs how a person is spoken to, not what is decided about their risk level.**

# **6\. Empirical Grounding Note (NEW in v1.1)**

This section closes the Empirical Grounding gate identified by 3-of-4 MIEVM nodes (DeepSeek, Gemini, ChatGPT) in the v1.0 review.

Stated plainly: the Enneagram’s standing as a psychometric instrument is contested in the academic and clinical literature. It does not carry the validation profile of instruments such as the Big Five or PHQ-9, and this instrument does not claim otherwise.

This protocol does not rely on the Enneagram’s predictive or diagnostic validity. Its function here is narrower and more defensible: a self-chosen continuity token — a way for a caller to say “remember this part of me” without re-explaining their situation at every contact. That function requires only that the caller recognizes themselves in it; it does not require that the framework be scientifically validated as a general personality taxonomy.

*Falsifiable test, to be run once Layer 1 is operational: track whether twin use correlates with (a) shorter time-to-recognition on repeat contact and (b) caller-reported sense of being known, against a control group offered a plain free-text continuity note with no Enneagram framing. If the Enneagram-specific framing shows no advantage over the plain control, this instrument is revised to drop the Enneagram layer in favor of the plainer record — the continuity function survives; the specific framing does not have to.*

# **7\. Layer 3 — Escalation Logic (Exposure-as-Liberation)**

The colloquial framing — “willing to go to jail, so to speak, in order to be set free” — names an internal design principle, not a literal consequence, and it is never to be presented to a caller in that language. Escalation is always opt-in, always reversible, and always paced by the caller.

# **8\. Non-Negotiable Integration Requirement**

**This instrument may not operate as a stand-alone substitute for licensed crisis response. At every point of contact, the live responder must have a direct, immediate handoff to 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, Crisis Text Line, or local emergency services. No version of L2 or L3 may be built, tested, or deployed ahead of confirming this handoff path is live and tested.**

# **10\. Public-Facing Messaging Constraint**

No caller-facing material may use incarceration, punishment, or “jail” language in any form. Public messaging should use plain, warm language — for example: “you can tell us the whole truth here, and nothing bad will happen to you for it.”
