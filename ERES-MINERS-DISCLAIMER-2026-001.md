# **Section 5: Anti-Optimizations — What NOT to Do**

The three foundational principles prohibit certain behaviors that might appear to optimize yield but actually violate the architecture’s moral constraints. These are anti-optimizations: actions that produce short-term metric improvement at the cost of genuine resonance.

## **5.1 Do Not Game Your Sensors**

Deliberate manipulation of biosensor data (e.g., taking stimulants before HRV measurement, artificially controlling breathing during measurement windows, using someone else’s data) produces false ΔResonance. MIEVM multi-instrument validation is designed to detect statistical anomalies inconsistent with genuine improvement. Fraudulent data will eventually be identified and flagged, resulting in credential suspension under CBGMODD state-transition governance. More importantly: you are only fooling yourself. The system exists to help you improve. Gaming it means you do not improve. Don’t hurt yourself.

## **5.2 Do Not Mine for Meritcoin**

This sounds paradoxical but is the most important anti-optimization. If your primary motivation is Meritcoin accumulation, you will optimize for metrics rather than genuine health, learning, and contribution. Meritcoin is a byproduct of resonance. Resonance is a byproduct of genuine improvement. Genuine improvement is a byproduct of caring about your own wellbeing and the wellbeing of others. The causal chain runs: caring → improvement → resonance → Meritcoin. If you start at the end (Meritcoin) and try to work backward, you get gaming, not mining. Mine for yourself. Meritcoin follows.

## **5.3 Do Not Sacrifice Sleep for Curriculum**

Level progression (Section 4, Optimization \#5) increases your Cert\_Level multiplier. Sleep optimization (Optimization \#1) increases your base ΔResonance. If you sacrifice sleep to accelerate curriculum completion, you destroy your base while increasing your multiplier. A 5.0x multiplier on a degraded ΔResonance yields less than a 1.5x multiplier on excellent ΔResonance. Protect your base. The curriculum will wait. Your circadian rhythm will not.

## **5.4 Do Not Compare Your Resonance to Others**

In Bitcoin, miners compete for block rewards. In Meritcoin, miners compete only against their own previous state. Your ΔResonance is measured against your own baseline, not against a global leaderboard. A miner who starts with poor health and improves moderately generates more Meritcoin (relative to their starting point) than a miner who starts with excellent health and improves marginally. The system rewards effort and improvement, not absolute performance. Comparison is not only unhelpful—it is structurally irrelevant to your yield.

## **5.5 Do Not Neglect the Non-Health Pillars**

The full TETRA CERT multiplier (5.0x at Level 401\) requires engagement with all four pillars: HEALTH, LAW, PROTECTION, and SKILLS/TRADE. A miner who perfects their SSHP (HEALTH) score but ignores SSLA (LAW), SSPS (PROTECTION), and SSST (SKILLS/TRADE) caps their multiplier at 2.0x. The architecture is designed holistically: the healthiest person who does not understand their legal rights, cannot protect their data, and cannot contribute productive skills is not fully resonant. Complete the TETRA. That is the optimization.
