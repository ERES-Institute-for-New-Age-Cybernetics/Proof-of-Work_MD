## Overview

**Meritcoin** is the merit-accrual unit of the ERES economic architecture. **Gracechain** is the ledger that records resonant contribution across the PlayNAC Empirical Realtime Education System. Together they form the economic layer of the 1,000-Year Future Map, paired with **UBIMIA** (Universal Basic Income + Merit, Investment, Awards) and **SROC** (Smart-Resonant Offset Contracts).

## Proof of Resonance

Meritcoin does **not** run on Proof-of-Work. It runs on **Proof-of-Resonance**: BERA indices (ARI, ERI, RHC, RCI) feed value accrual based on measurable harmonic contribution.

> **"It's not mining — it's tuning."**

| BERA Index | Full Name | Role |
|------------|-----------|------|
| ARI | Aura Resonance Index | Personal resonance measurement |
| ERI | Environmental Resonance Index | Ecological resonance measurement |
| RHC | Resonant Harmony Cycle | Temporal coherence of the system |
| RCI | Resonant Coherence Index | `RCI = P_Ω_norm × ARI_sys × VibConst` |

The RCI formula was co-developed with **Jimmy D. Butzbach**'s Continuity Theory.
