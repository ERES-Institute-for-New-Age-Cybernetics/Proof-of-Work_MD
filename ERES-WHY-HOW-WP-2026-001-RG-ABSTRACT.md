## Abstract (≈ 290 words)

This paper encapsulates the *why* of the ERES Institute's New Age Cybernetic Game Theory with the *how* of its operational substrate by demonstrating that agriculture is the only economic sector capable of generating positive Proof-of-Resonance under the PlayNAC ruleset, and is therefore the natural — not legislated — offset to the fear-based death economics of the war machine. The argument is arithmetic, not moral. Under conventional GDP accounting, destruction registers as activity and therefore as value; under the GCF / NBERS ledger, destruction registers as negative resonance and therefore as Meritcoin debt. The war machine does not fail ethically under PlayNAC — it fails to score.

Agriculture, by contrast, satisfies all four BERA indices (ARI, ERI, RHC, RCI) at both substrate and cellular scales. Soil microbial diversity corresponds to ARI; photosynthetic efficiency and trophic transfer to ERI; growing-season and fallow rotations to the Resonant Harmony Cycle; year-over-year soil carbon accrual and seed-line continuity to RCI. The GSSG Sugar-Carbon Reservoir Hypothesis links soil carbon directly to human metabolic coherence, unifying the Three Governing Principles — *don't hurt yourself, don't hurt others, build for generations to come* — as one principle expressed at three scales (cellular, social, planetary), all three of which regenerative agriculture satisfies simultaneously.

The VLSA Principle confirms agriculture as the only sector structurally identical from a Tiny House on Wheels (THOW) to a Forward Deployable Resilient Vessel (FDRV); it is the load-bearing activity that survives the 1000-year journey. Under Global Earth Resource Planning (GERP), the resulting capital flow from war to agriculture is hydraulic rather than political: PlayNAC re-grades the slope of the ledger, and the bio-ecologic offset emerges as the natural drainage pattern of an economy whose accounting has been re-scored to Proof-of-Resonance.
