Ability in the ERES framework is not abstract competence. It is demonstrated, credentialed, gamified, and continuously verified performance within a system that simultaneously educates, evaluates, governs, plans resources, responds to emergencies, and coordinates stakeholders across the seven-layer CBGMODD structure (Citizen, Business, Government, Military, Ombudsman, Dignitary, Diplomat).
