## Abstract

This white paper defines the data‑mining and feedback architecture of the *Empirical Realtime Education System (ERES)* as the foundational intelligence governing the **1000‑Year Future Map**.  
Rooted in **New Age Cybernetics (NAC)**, ERES unifies adaptive education, regenerative economics, and planetary governance through recursive observation and incentive alignment.  
An *As‑Is / To‑Be Conversion Model* quantifies civilization‑scale transformation across sociocratic, cybernetic dimensions.

## 4. Data‑Mining Workflow

1. **Input Acquisition:** Collect sensorial, cognitive, transactional, and ecological data via all modules.  
2. **Normalization:** Convert heterogeneous inputs to **SOMT**‑encoded metadata.  
3. **Semantic Mining:** Apply pattern recognition to reveal ethical coherence and entropy gradients.  
4. **Feedback Injection:** Update PlayNAC and GERP parameters in near‑realtime.  
5. **Projection:** Generate Future‑Map nodes and transformation coefficients for each domain.
