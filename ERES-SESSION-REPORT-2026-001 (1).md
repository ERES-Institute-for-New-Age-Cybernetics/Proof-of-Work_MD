# **3\. MIT Press Prospectus**

The session began with a structured publisher selection process. MIT Press was selected based on three factors: (a) the existing Engineering Systems series established by Prof. Olivier L. De Weck, which provides the closest theoretical frame to the ERES approach; (b) the MIT Press Editorial Board’s domain coverage, which maps directly to the ERES Trilogy; and (c) the Direct to Open program for open-access monographs, which aligns with CCAL v2.1 licensing.
