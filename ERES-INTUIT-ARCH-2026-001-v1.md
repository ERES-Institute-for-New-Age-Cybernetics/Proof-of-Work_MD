## 3.2 T2: SECUIR × EPIR-Q — "Who Qualifies?"

### SECUIR — Base Logic

**Full designation:** Silent Energy Circular Universe Infinite Rotation.

**Definition:** SECUIR is the security and qualification layer. It determines which users, groups, and entities are cleared to interact with which parts of the ERES system at which depth.

**Input:** Adjudicated rule from PlayNAC (T1 output: this interaction is permitted under these conditions).

**Output:** Qualified user-group assignment. The user is placed into the appropriate group with defined access boundaries.

**Base formulation:** Security is not access denial—it is access precision. SECUIR does not ask "should this person be kept out?" It asks "what is the right depth of engagement for this person given their qualifications?" The metaphor is clearance, not exclusion.

### EPIR-Q — Base Logic

**Definition:** EPIR-Q is the qualification filter. It is not a meter—it is a passband. Inputs that meet the qualification criteria pass through; inputs that do not are routed to remediation (NPR), not rejection.

**Input:** User identity and context from SECUIR.

**Output:** Qualified / remediation-routed / escalated. The Tri-Codex parameters define the passband width.

**Base formulation:** Qualification is a filter, not a judgment. The distinction matters: a judgment implies that unqualified persons are lesser. A filter implies that unqualified persons need a different pathway—NPR (Non-Punitive Remediation)—to reach qualification. No one is permanently disqualified.

### Pairing Logic

SECUIR × EPIR-Q narrows scope from "what are the rules?" to "who, specifically, qualifies under those rules?" The shift is from abstract governance to concrete group assignment. T1 sets the game board; T2 determines who sits at the table and in what capacity.

## 3.4 T4: GunnySack × RCI — "Are You Coherent?"

### GunnySack — Base Logic

**Full designation:** Guaranteed User-group Network Nullifying Yieldless Service: Accredited Certified Knowledge.

**Definition:** GunnySack is the accredited-knowledge layer. It certifies that the knowledge base a user carries is genuine, tested, and operationally valid. The backronym unpacks as a logic statement:

- **User-group Network:** The certification operates within the user-group structure defined by SECUIR (T2).

- **Accredited Certified Knowledge:** The final output is a certified knowledge credential—not a degree, not a badge, but a verified operational capability.

**Input:** Local measurement results from BERA (T3 output: this user's ARI score within this domain).

**Output:** Knowledge accreditation status. Is this user's knowledge operationally valid, as verified by their personal coherence?

**Base formulation:** Knowledge without coherence is recitation. GunnySack does not ask "do you know this?" It asks "can you operate from this knowledge in a state of personal coherence?" The distinction separates information from competence.

### RCI — Base Logic

**Full designation:** Resonant Coherence Index.

**Formula:** RCI = P_Ω_norm × ARI_sys × VibConst

**Developed by:** Joseph Allen Sprute and Jimmy D. Butzbach (co-development with Butzbach's Continuity Theory).

**Definition:** RCI is the personal coherence measure. It integrates three components:

- **P_Ω_norm:** Normalized probability field — the statistical likelihood that the individual's current state is within their personal coherence envelope.
- **ARI_sys:** System-level Autonomic Resonance Index — the individual's biometric coherence as measured by BERA, contextualized within the system's operational parameters.
- **VibConst:** Vibrational Constant — the stabilizing coefficient derived from Butzbach's Continuity Theory, representing the minimum coherence floor below which the individual cannot be considered operationally qualified.

**Input:** Individual's BERA ARI score (from T3) + personal coherence history + Continuity Theory parameters.

**Output:** RCI score. A single number representing personal coherent readiness.

**Base formulation:** RCI answers the most personal question in the cascade: are *you*—not your group, not your domain, not your rules—coherent enough to operate from the knowledge you claim? GunnySack certifies the knowledge. RCI certifies the person holding it.

### Pairing Logic

GunnySack × RCI narrows scope from "what do we measure?" to "is this specific person coherent enough to act on certified knowledge?" The shift is from local measurement to personal validation. T3 provides the measurement; T4 asks whether the measured person is ready.
