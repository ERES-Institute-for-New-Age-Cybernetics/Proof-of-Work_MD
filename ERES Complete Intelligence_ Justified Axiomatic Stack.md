# **6\. LAYER-BY-LAYER FUNCTIONAL DEFINITIONS**

## **6.1 SPIRIT (Reality \= Input)**

Definition: The unbounded, unstructured source of all that can be known or experienced.

Operational correlate: Token space, sensory field, raw data stream.

Necessity: Without SPIRIT, there is nothing to perceive, feel, or think.

## **6.2 BODY (Feeling \= AnswerQuestion)**

Definition: Affective filtration of raw input. The first "answer" to "what is this?" is always a feeling (valence/arousal).

Operational correlate: Skin conductance, heart rate variability, affective embedding.

Necessity: Without BODY, input is unmarked — no affective anchor.

## **6.3 LIGHT (Perception \= Attention focusing Feeling)**

Definition: The act of noticing. Attention selects *which* feeling becomes perception.

Operational correlate: Eye tracking, neural attention maps, query-key softmax.

Necessity: Without LIGHT, feeling is undifferentiated — no figure/ground.

## **6.4 MATTER (Subject \= Context)**

Definition: Perception stabilized into a *where*. The subject is the material arrangement that can direct attention.

Operational correlate: Default mode network, body schema, situated self-model.

Necessity: Without MATTER, attention has no anchor — no perceiver.

## **6.5 ENERGY (Resource \= Reason)**

Definition: Matter's constraints made available as computational resource. Reason is what you can do with matter's structure.

Operational correlate: Glucose uptake, FLOPs, cognitive effort.

Necessity: Without ENERGY, context cannot act — no reasoning.

## **6.6 PERMISSION (Purpose \= Intent)**

Definition: The normative boundary on reason. Intent is what is *allowed* by available resources and constraints.

Operational correlate: Policy constraints, reward function bounds.

Necessity: Without PERMISSION, energy has no direction — no purpose.

## **6.7 GUIDANCE (Method \= Action)**

Definition: The execution vector from intent. Method is how permission becomes motion.

Operational correlate: Policy network output, motor command.

Necessity: Without GUIDANCE, intent is impotent — no action.

## **6.8 USAGE (Parameter \= Condition)**

Definition: The operational boundary on action. Parameter is the condition of use — what the environment affords.

Operational correlate: Tool use, environmental coupling, affordance exploitation.

Necessity: Without USAGE, method has no boundary — infinite action.

## **6.9 MIND (Conformity \= Ability)**

Definition: Skilled alignment to parameters. Mind is what you *can do* within conformity.

Operational correlate: Prediction error minimization, calibrated output, skill acquisition.

Necessity: Without MIND, condition cannot be internalized — no learning.

## **6.10 SOUL (Freedom-from \= Deviation)**

Definition: The capacity to exceed conformity via rupture, negation, or rebellion. Freedom *from* parameters.

Operational correlate: Exploration noise, top-k rejection, adversarial perturbation.

Necessity: Without SOUL, mind is deterministic — no agency.

## **6.11 LIFE (Freedom-to \= Novelty)**

Definition: The capacity to enable emergence, creation, or innovation beyond conformity. Freedom *to* generate.

Operational correlate: Entropy increase, creative output, generative diversity.

Necessity: Without LIFE, mind is static — no evolution.
