# **1\. Executive Summary**

UBIMIA is the ERES economic operating layer that pairs an unconditional universal floor with merit-recognized augmentation, settled across two complementary ledgers: Gracechain (the unconditional, gift-economy ledger) and Meritcoin (the contribution-recognized, Proof-of-Resonance ledger). Together they replace extractive Proof-of-Work with a tuning model — value enters the economy when human and ecological systems come into measurable resonance, not when energy is wasted on arbitrary computation.

This summary defines the components, shows how they interlock, and situates UBIMIA inside the broader ERES framework already filed at The Hague (ERES-HAGUE-2026-001) and developed across 424 ResearchGate publications — a peer-visible archive supporting continuity of development.

# **2\. UBIMIA — The Two-Layer Economic Floor**

UBIMIA stands for Universal Basic Income \+ Meritocratic Income Augmentation. It is a single, integrated entitlement architecture with two functionally distinct layers:

* **Layer A — UBI (the Floor):** an unconditional, lifelong allowance that secures food, shelter, healthcare, and learning access for every recognized participant. Distribution occurs on Gracechain and is treated in ERES as a human right, not a transfer payment.

* **Layer B — MIA (the Augmentation):** a merit-tracked supplement earned through verified contribution to SELF, OTHERS, and FUTURE. MIA accrues in Meritcoin, is computed from BERA — the four-index Bio-Ecological Resonance Architecture (ARI, ERI, RHC, RCI) — and converts into purchasing power, governance weight, and access tier under SCALULAR (the universal service-tier pricing layer covering HEALTH, LAW, PROTECTION, and SKILLS\_TRADE).

The architectural insight is that the two layers must coexist. A floor without augmentation collapses incentive; augmentation without a floor collapses dignity. UBIMIA refuses that trade-off by routing each function through a ledger purpose-built for it.

# **3\. Gracechain — The Unconditional Ledger**

Gracechain is the settlement layer for grace: gifts, baseline entitlements, restorative transfers, and intergenerational endowments. It carries the UBI portion of UBIMIA and any value movement that should occur regardless of merit recognition.

## **Design properties**

* Non-conditional issuance — receipt does not require prior contribution, productivity scoring, or behavioral compliance.

* Recipient-anchored identity — bound to the person rather than to wallet history; loss of credentials does not strand the floor (recovery via independent trustees).

* Restorative settlement — the channel through which Patriot Dividend, FAVORS rebalancing, and IDIPITIS adjustments flow.

* Gift-economy semantics — transactions are recorded but not collateralized; Gracechain does not extend interest-bearing debt.

Gracechain answers the question every UBI program eventually faces: how do you guarantee the floor without making the floor itself a coercion lever? By isolating unconditional issuance on its own ledger, ERES prevents the merit ledger from being weaponized to revoke survival.

# **4\. Meritcoin — Proof-of-Resonance**

Meritcoin is the contribution-recognized currency of MIA. It is mined by tuning, not by hashing. The minting condition is a measurable improvement in resonance across the four BERA indices:

* ARI — Adaptive Resonance Index (system fit to changing conditions)

* ERI — Ethical Resonance Index (alignment with the Eight Immutable Ethical Principles)

* RHC — Resonant Harmony Cycle (sustained periodic coherence over time)

* RCI — Resonance Continuity Index, computed as P\_Ω\_norm × ARI\_sys × VibConst (co-developed with Jimmy D. Butzbach's Continuity Theory)

The operative slogan is deliberate: ***“It's not mining — it's tuning.”*** Where Bitcoin externalizes cost as heat, Meritcoin internalizes value as harmony.

What prevents fraud and gaming. Minting is gated by the ERES Attestation and Authorization Protocol (EAAP) — a conjunctive product across four independent factors, including MIEVM (Multi-Instrument Ensemble Validation Method) concurrence that no single party can self-certify — combined with oracle-measured system change before and after the contribution, randomized audits, and reputation-weighted validation. Meritcoin balance is irrelevant to access pricing; only contribution history matters. Hoarding accomplishes nothing. Meritcoin cannot be exchanged for fiat — earned only, spent only — closing the leak through which extractive capital re-enters.

# **5\. Conversion: Resonance-Priced Access**

Meritcoin converts to SCALULAR service access under a fixed, non-auctioned formula:

**Cost \= BaseCost(Tier) × Complexity(Service) × 1 / (1 \+ ΔR\_contributor)**

ΔR\_contributor is the participant's normalized 12-month resonance contribution history — flow, not stock. Higher contribution lowers the price (down to a 50% floor). Balance never appears in the formula. No auctions, no bidding, no wealth-based exclusion. Annual settlement converts unspent Meritcoin into Credits (60% carried forward, 40% to a Community Opportunity Investment pool that reduces base costs for everyone the following year).

# **7\. How the Components Interlock**

UBIMIA is the policy. Gracechain and Meritcoin are the rails. Every recognized person receives a continuous Gracechain credit (the UBI floor); every verified contribution clears EAAP authorization and registers a Meritcoin event (the MIA augmentation); SCALULAR services price themselves against the Resonance-Priced Access formula. Basic survival never depends on merit accrual, while excellence is durably rewarded. The same account, two ledgers, one person — and no scenario in which a citizen falls through the floor because their contribution score dipped or because a regime transition was detected.
