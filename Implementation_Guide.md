### Paper Formatting for ResearchGate

Each paper requires:

1. **PDF Version** (required by ResearchGate)
   - Professional typography
   - Consistent styling
   - Page numbers
   - Headers/footers
   - Copyright notice

2. **Metadata**
   - Title (exact match to paper)
   - Authors: ERES Institute for New Age Cybernetics / Joseph A. Sprute
   - Publication date
   - DOI (if available)
   - Keywords (5-10 per paper)
   - Abstract (150-300 words)

3. **Supplementary Files**
   - High-resolution figures
   - Data sets (if applicable)
   - Code repositories (link to GitHub)
   - Appendices

"Thank you for your thoughtful question about [topic]. Let me clarify by explaining [explanation]. For more detail, please see [section] of the paper or [related resource]. Does this address your question?"
