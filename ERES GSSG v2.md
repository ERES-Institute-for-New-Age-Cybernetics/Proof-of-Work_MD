### **2.1 Graphene Base Preparation**

* Method: Electrochemical exfoliation (green, low-acid, 0.1 M (NH₄)₂SO₄) or biomass/CO₂-derived CVD (methane from captured CO₂, 1000°C, Ni/Cu catalyst)  
* Target: Roll-to-roll sheets ≥100 m², 10–100 μm thick (single-layer to few-layer graphene)  
* Quality control:  
  * Sheet resistance mapping (4-point probe; σ \> 10⁵ S/m uniformity ±5%)  
  * Piezoelectric coupling coefficient (d₃₃ at 7.83 Hz via lock-in amplifier; target \>10 pC/N)  
  * Raman spectroscopy (G/D ratio \>20; 2D peak symmetric)

### **2.2 GSSG Integration**

* Aqueous dispersion: Graphene oxide (GO, 1–5 mg/mL) \+ GSSG (0.1–1 mg/mL) in deoxygenated phosphate buffer (pH 7.4, 10 mM) → sonication (40 kHz, 30 min) under N₂ atmosphere

* Bonding verification:  
  * XPS (S 2p peak for disulfide at 163–164 eV; C-S peak at 285.5 eV)  
  * Raman (G/D ratio retention \>20; no defect introduction)  
  * Electrochemical impedance (charge transfer resistance \< 100 Ω; redox peak separation \< 100 mV)

* Deposition: Spray-coating (0.1–1 mL/min, 50°C substrate), dip-coating (withdrawal rate 1 mm/s), or electrophoretic layering (10 V/cm, 10 min) on substrates  
* Redox capacity calibration:  
  * Open-circuit potential vs. Ag/AgCl (target 0.15–0.25 V for as-prepared)
