**Abstract**

This paper formalizes the ERES Cryptographic Stack Primitive at version 1.3, locking the resonance-keyed semantic superposition primitive class as the foundational cryptographic substrate for ERES Institute architectures. The primitive is expressed by the canonical relation **(CBGMODD × FAVORS) \+ BERA \= RATE**, where seven RATE dimensions (R₁–R₇) are derived from a three-tier semantic stack: Symbols (atomic glyph layer), Characters (compositional identity layer), and Talonics (governance-state layer, 18×7 \= 126 states across 12 parts and 452 elements). Version 1.3 introduces the **VEILED** state as a principled replacement for the conventional bottom value (⊥), preserving epistemic incompleteness without collapse into falsity. It additionally formalizes **Trifurcated Math** as the third pedagogical isomorph of Triune Math \#2, completing the family of canonical keys: (1) C \= R × P / M; (2) M × E \+ C \= R; (3) REAL \= (E · M · R) / (T · S). The primitive is the attestation substrate for SCALULAR and the upstream dependency for EAAP v1.2.

## **1\. Introduction**

The ERES Cryptographic Stack Primitive is not a hash function, not a signature scheme, and not a key-exchange protocol. It is the *semantic substrate* on which those operational primitives are layered. Conventional cryptography assumes meaning is exogenous to the cryptographic object — a signed bit-string is a signed bit-string, regardless of what the bits represent. The ERES Stack Primitive inverts that assumption: meaning is endogenous, carried by a three-tier semantic stack whose composition rules generate cryptographically usable structure.  
Versions 1.0 through 1.2 established the layered stack and the operational relation. Version 1.3 closes three open items: (i) the VEILED state, (ii) Trifurcated Math, and (iii) the formal class definition of resonance-keyed semantic superposition.

## **2\. The Three-Tier Stack**

**2.1 Symbols.** The atomic glyph layer. Symbols carry no compositional commitments; they are addressable, enumerable, and distinguishable. The Symbol layer is closed under identity but not under combination.  
**2.2 Characters.** The compositional identity layer. Characters are constructed from Symbols under named composition rules and acquire stable identity across contexts. A Character is the smallest unit at which an attestable claim can be made.  
**2.3 Talonics.** The governance-state layer. The Talonics Symbol Matrix consists of 452 elements organized into 12 parts, generating 18 × 7 \= 126 governance states. Each Talonic state encodes a compositional commitment about how Characters may be combined under a given governance regime. Talonics are the layer at which cryptographic operations acquire *normative* (not merely syntactic) force.

## **3\. The Canonical Relation**

The operational relation of the stack is:  
**(CBGMODD × FAVORS) \+ BERA \= RATE**  
Where:

* **CBGMODD** — the seven-seat Family Stewardship Council membership vector (Caretakers, Beneficiaries, Guardians, Mediators, Officers, Directors, Delegates).  
* **FAVORS** — the multidimensional compatibility lattice between participating roles and proposed actions.  
* **BERA** — the Behavioral and Environmental Resonance Audit, a measurement framework producing scalar and vector outputs across ARI, ERI, RHC, and RCI dimensions.  
* **RATE** — the seven-dimensional output (R₁–R₇), each dimension keyed to a distinct attestation surface.

**3.1 The seven RATE dimensions:**

* **R₁ — Receipt:** the cryptographic acknowledgment that the relation has executed.  
* **R₂ — Reach:** the scope of CBGMODD seats whose authority is engaged.  
* **R₃ — Resonance:** the BERA-derived scalar at moment of attestation.  
* **R₄ — Resilience:** the projected stability of the attestation under adversarial perturbation.  
* **R₅ — Reciprocity:** the FAVORS-derived obligation vector returned to participating roles.  
* **R₆ — Record:** the immutable trace deposited to the attestation substrate.  
* **R₇ — Right:** the normative claim — under the active Talonics state — that the attestation confers.

## **4\. The VEILED State**

In conventional logic, ⊥ denotes falsity, contradiction, or the empty model. In cryptographic protocols, ⊥ frequently overloads to denote "no value," "abort," or "rejection." This overloading is corrosive in attestation contexts: an absent attestation, a withheld attestation, and a denied attestation are not the same epistemic event.  
Version 1.3 introduces **VEILED** as a principled replacement for ⊥ in the ERES Stack Primitive. A VEILED state asserts: *the Right (R₇) is not currently displayed at this attestation surface, but the underlying relation has not collapsed.* VEILED preserves epistemic incompleteness without forcing it into falsity. It is recoverable under proper credentialing; ⊥ is not. VEILED is the formal accommodation, at the cryptographic layer, of the IDIPITIS principle that not all knowledge is concurrently disclosed knowledge.

## **5\. Trifurcated Math as Third Pedagogical Isomorph**

Triune Math is taught in three pedagogical isomorphs:

* **Key 1 — Cybernetic form:** C \= R × P / M (Cybernetics \= Resource × Purpose ÷ Method).  
* **Key 2 — Physical form:** M × E \+ C \= R (Matter × Energy \+ Constant \= Reason).  
* **Key 3 — Resonance form:** REAL \= (E · M · R) / (T · S) (Energy · Matter · Resonance over Time · Space; no repeated letters).

**Trifurcated Math** is the third pedagogical isomorph *of Key 2*, presenting M × E \+ C \= R as a branching structure that exposes the three contributions (Matter, Energy, Constant) as separable but co-determinative inputs to Reason. Trifurcated Math is the bridge between Triune Math's algebraic compactness and the Talonics layer's compositional richness; it is what makes the cryptographic primitive teachable to non-specialist participants.

## **6\. The Primitive Class**

The **resonance-keyed semantic superposition** primitive class is defined as the family of cryptographic objects O such that:

1. O is constructed over a three-tier semantic stack (Symbols / Characters / Talonics).  
2. O's operational relation is of the form (council × compatibility) \+ audit \= output, with output decomposed into seven attestation dimensions.  
3. O admits a VEILED state distinct from rejection.  
4. O's pedagogical exposition is consistent with the three Triune Math isomorphs and Trifurcated Math.

Any cryptographic object meeting these four criteria is a member of the class and inherits the attestation guarantees of the ERES Stack Primitive.
