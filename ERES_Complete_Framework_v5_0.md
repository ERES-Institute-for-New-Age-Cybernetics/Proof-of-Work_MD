### **Formula 1: C = R × P / M**
**Cybernetics = Resource × Purpose / Method**

### **Formula 2: M × E + C = R**
**Matter × Energy + Cybernetics = Resonance**

**Purpose:** Achieves equilibrium through transformation  
**Application:** Resolves conflicts and designs interventions

### **Formula 3: REAL = (E · M · R) / (T · S)**
**Resonant Energy About Love = (Energy · Matter · Resonance) / (Time · Space)**

**Purpose:** Measures sustainability in spacetime  
**Application:** Verifies that solutions actually work across generations

**Every ERES intervention follows this sequence:**

1. **Formula 1 calculates** required governance intervention (C) based on available resources (R), purpose alignment (P), and actor merit (M)

3. **Formula 3 verifies** that the achieved resonance (R) is sustainable across the required time (T) and space (S) dimensions
