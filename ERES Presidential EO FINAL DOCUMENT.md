### **PART II — THE PATRIOT DIVIDEND PROGRAM**

\**Section 4\. Establishment of the Patriot Dividend.* \*

(a) There is hereby established the Patriot Dividend Program, a strategic, time‑bound direct economic transfer to every verified American adult during periods of declared national economic emergency.

(b) The Patriot Dividend is not welfare. (c) The Secretary of the Treasury, in coordination with the Director of DOFA, shall establish the Patriot Dividend Fund. (d) The monthly Dividend amount shall be determined using the Consumer Crisis Index (CCI), targeting an 80 percent offset of essential‑goods inflation for the median American household.

(a) Citizens who voluntarily return part or all of their Patriot Dividend shall receive Patriot Credits at a 1.25x multiplier for the first 100 percent of the monthly Dividend returned, and 1.5x for sustained participation of six or more consecutive months.

(b) Patriot Credits shall be non‑transferable, non‑cashable, and applicable across five channels: (1) Tax Credit against Federal income tax liability; (2) Education Credit at any accredited institution or SCALULAR‑certified training module; (3) Housing Credit toward down payment, rent, or mortgage; (4) Healthcare Credit for qualified medical expenses; and (5) Merit Badge recognition in the EarnedPath civic ledger.

(d) No citizen shall be penalized, disadvantaged, or subjected to reduced services for choosing not to participate in the Voluntary Return Mechanism. The system grows through attraction, not compulsion.

\**Section 6\. Sunset and Transition Provisions.* \*

(a) The Patriot Dividend shall terminate 90 days after all of the following conditions have been sustained for 90 consecutive days: (1) the triggering national economic emergency has been formally rescinded; (2) the relevant commodity index is within 115 percent of the pre‑crisis average; and (3) essential‑goods CPI is below 3.0 percent annualized.
