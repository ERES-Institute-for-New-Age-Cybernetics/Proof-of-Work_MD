## About This Repository

**Proof-of-Work** is the **Genesis Archive** of the ERES Institute for New Age Cybernetics — the original collection of foundational documents, vision statements, partnership proposals, applied frameworks, and tangential research from which the entire ERES repository ecosystem grew. These are the seed documents: the first articulations of what later became the formalized Trilogy (BEST/SOUND/GOOD), the BERA resonance metrics, the SCALULAR certification architecture, and the ESVRD propulsion framework.

This archive represents the raw thinking — unrevised, sometimes provisional, always authentic — that preceded the structured production sprints of 2025–2026. Many of these documents have been superseded by newer, formalized versions in other repositories, but they remain here as proof-of-work: timestamped evidence that the ideas existed before the refinement.

**11 commits · 50+ PDF documents · The seedbed of a 13-year program**

---

## Why This Archive Matters

In the ERES epistemology, proof-of-work is not computational — it is existential. These documents prove that the NAC framework was not reverse-engineered from a single moment of clarity but emerged through sustained, iterative, sometimes messy effort across years. A reviewer, publisher, or collaborator encountering the ERES corpus for the first time can trace any current concept back to its genesis-layer expression here.

The Covenant with Humanity alone exists in four versions (v1 → v2 → v3.9 → v4.0), each one a timestamp in the moral evolution of the framework. The BEST Biometric Checkout series shows the applied thinking that eventually fed into IDIPITIS. The 1000-Year Future Map drafts are the ancestors of the SPT Papers' VLSA scale proof.

> *This is the sprout — SPRUTE (SPRU-TE) — from which the tree grew.*
