## 0. Purpose

This protocol issues a single, narrow certificate: that a within-subject's
**cross-channel physiological coherence** shifts measurably and repeatably in
response to a defined change in environmental **sound**.

It is the GOOD-layer (Data-Integrity) handshake for resonance measurement —
one concrete instantiation of the generic `GOOD → SOUND → BEST` Trinity
interface. The **Environmental input** section (§3) is the only domain-specific
part. Swapping it yields Instance 02 (light/circadian) and Instance 03
(controlled odorant) with everything else unchanged.

---

## 1. Claim under test

> A single subject's cross-channel physiological coherence tracks a defined
> change in environmental sound, carrying information that no single channel
> carries alone.

**Certificate issued on pass:** *"This coherence reading is real and tracks the
environmental input."* — Data-Integrity only. Nothing above this rung.

---

## 2. What this protocol does NOT claim

- It does **not** claim the coherence indexes a feeling.
- It does **not** claim it indexes a relationship, compatibility, or "who you
  can live with."
- It does **not** claim it indexes a personality type (Enneagram or otherwise).

Those are higher rungs (between-person synchrony; compatibility/type). Each
requires its own chain of integrity beneath it. **The Infomediary qualifying
any AnswerQuestion from this instrument may qualify only to the GOOD level —
"integral reading" — and no further.** Over-qualification is the leap this
protocol exists to prevent.

## 3. Environmental input — the knob *(only domain-specific section)*

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Variable | Ambient sound |
| Condition A | Calibrated silence (controlled low-dB baseline) |
| Condition B | Pink noise, ~60 dB SPL, fixed amplitude and spectrum |
| Measurement | Calibrated dB + spectrum log **at the room** (never inferred) |

Rationale: pink noise is spectrally neutral to the ear and carries no semantic,
musical, or valence content — it isolates *sound presence vs. silence*. Music
is excluded: it smuggles in familiarity and emotional valence, the same
uncontrolled-variable problem as using "feelings" as an input. Sound is the
first instance because it is the most switchable knob — conditions alternate in
seconds with no residue.

## 4. Subject channels — the body (four measured streams)

| Channel | Measure | Sampling rate |
|---|---|---|
| Cardiac | HRV (chest strap, or validated PPG w/ R-peak detection) | 1000 Hz |
| Respiration | Rate + depth (band, or ECG-derived) | 25 Hz |
| Electrodermal | Skin conductance (EDA) | 4 Hz (min) |
| Odor / VOC | Gas-sensor array, subject's local air | Continuous, native |

Three channels is the minimum for "coherence" to mean more than a pairwise
correlation. The fourth (VOC) is included because it is also one of the body's
emission channels and earns its place in the resonance architecture.

---

## 5. Design

- **Type:** Within-subject, repeated alternation.
- **Baseline:** 5-minute seated rest before block 1 (settling period; standard).
- **Blocks:** `A – B – A – B – A – B`, six blocks, **5 minutes each**.
- **Block length basis:** the short-term HRV standard (Task Force, 5 min) — set
  by the slowest-resolving channel; shorter windows make the HRV spectral
  estimate unreliable and weaken the coherence computation at its limiting
  channel.
- **Total session:** 5 min baseline + 30 min blocks = **35 minutes**.
- **Counterbalancing:** block order counterbalanced across sessions.
- **Replication:** three full A/B pairs per session = within-session
  replication (the floor for SOUND-layer attestation — the result must repeat
  within the session before it earns clearance).
- **Control:** one variable moves (sound); everything else held constant.

---

## 6. Measurement — *this is the hypothesis*

Compute **cross-channel coherence** — the degree to which the four streams
co-vary / phase-lock over each block. Established methods: cross-spectral
coherence, phase-synchronization indices, cardiorespiratory coupling.

Compare the coherence pattern between Condition A and Condition B.

---

## 7. Falsification line — *this is what makes it a handshake, not a belief*

> **The study FAILS if cross-channel coherence does not separate Condition A
> from Condition B better than the single most informative channel does on its
> own.**

If one channel (e.g. EDA) distinguishes silence from sound as well as the whole
coherence pattern, there is no integrator — just one channel and noise. Passing
**requires the joint pattern to carry information the best single channel does
not.** The threshold and the comparison method are **pre-registered before data
collection.**

## 8. Handshake / Trinity mapping

| Layer | Anchor | Gate (must confirm before next layer proceeds) |
|---|---|---|
| **GOOD** | Data-Integrity · Baidu | Channel signals intact, time-synced, artifact-checked. *No coherence computed until this confirms — first handshake.* |
| **SOUND** | Security-Clearance · SPRU | Coherence result attested: pre-registered threshold, counterbalancing, within-session replication — before it may mean anything. |
| **BEST** | One-Good · MIT | Result resolves into a named ERES quantity. **RHC (Resonant Harmony Cycle) is the name BEST gives the within-person coherence result once the chain completes.** |

**Dependency (build) order:** Baidu → SPRU → MIT.
**The order is the invariant. The contents are polymorphic.** A handshake whose
steps could run in any order guarantees nothing — so the sequence is the
contract, even though the rung contents instantiate differently across domains.
