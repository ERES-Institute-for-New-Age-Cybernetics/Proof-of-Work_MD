## Purpose

A dedicated space for conversation about the ERES corpus — the PlayNAC KERNEL, BERA indices, Meritcoin economics, SCALULAR certification, FAVORS/CBGMODD governance, and everything adjacent. Q&A, proposals, implementation stories, critique, and open reflection all live here.

## Categories

| Category | Use For |
|----------|---------|
| **📣 Announcements** | Releases, filings, milestones (read-only to community) |
| **💡 Ideas & Proposals** | New framework extensions, term proposals, subsystem ideas |
| **❓ Q & A** | Questions about terminology, specifications, or implementation |
| **🔬 Research & Evidence** | Empirical data, MIEVM validation runs, reproductions |
| **🛠 Implementation** | Code, deployments, integrations with PlayNAC KERNEL |
| **📚 Corpus & Citations** | Discussion of papers, ResearchGate publications, prior art |
| **🗣 Show & Tell** | Projects, art, visuals, teaching materials |
| **🌍 Governance & Policy** | UBIMIA, Hague submission, Equity Covenant, Executive Order draft |

## Community Guidelines

Participation is governed by the **Three Governing Principles**:

1. **Don't hurt yourself** — no reckless disclosure of private data, credentials, or health information.
2. **Don't hurt others** — no harassment, doxing, or bad-faith provocation.
3. **Build for generations to come** — engage with work on its merits; critique freely, attack no one.

See [`CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) in [`ZIP`](../ZIP) for enforcement mechanics.

## Good-Faith Engagement

ERES terminology has long lineage. Before asserting that a term is wrong, check:

- **Locked Terms Registry** — ERES TERMS #47, in [`Support-Documentation`](../Support-Documentation).
- **Canonical corrections** — SOMT = Sociocratic Overlay Metadata Tapestry; SROC = Smart-Resonant Offset Contracts; RHC = Resonant Harmony **Cycle**; Meritcoin = Proof-of-**Resonance**.
- **Three Triune Math keys** — `C = R × P / M`, `M × E + C = R`, `REAL = (E·M·R) / (T·S)`.

Corrections are welcome; conflicts with earlier drafts are expected and resolved via the Locked Terms Registry.
