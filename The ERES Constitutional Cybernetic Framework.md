### **2.2 PILLAR II: NBERS (New Bio-Ecologic Rating System)**

NBERS replaces GDP with a holistic planetary prosperity metric measuring:

* Ecological health (biodiversity, carbon sequestration, water quality)

* Human well-being (mental health, life expectancy, community engagement)  
* Bio-energetic resonance through Aura Resonance Index (ARI)

The framework incorporates BERA (Bio-Energetic Resonance Assessment), BEST (Bio-Electric Signature Time), and EPIR-Q (Emotional Personal IQ Real and Quantum) measurement systems, enabling companies to be evaluated by contribution to actual well-being rather than market capitalization.

### **2.3 PILLAR III: FAVORS (Fingerprint, Aura, Voice, Odor, Retina, Signature)**

The governance structure employs CBGMODD (Citizen, Business, Government, Military, Ombudsman, Dignitary, Diplomat) stakeholder representation, coordinated by GAIA (Global Actuary Investor Authority) for planetary long-term planning. Computational infrastructure is provided by PlayNAC KERNEL—a governance operating system written in Rust/Solidity/Python/TypeScript—and the NAC Constitution living framework for 1000-year planning.
