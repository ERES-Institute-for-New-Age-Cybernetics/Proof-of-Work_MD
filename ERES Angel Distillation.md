## **THE DEAL FOR EACH ROLE**

### **Role 1: Money Handler (Steward)**

# *"You handle money. I never see it. You pay bills. I never ask. You send one sentence quarterly: 'Balance X. Spent Y.' That is the job."*

# Time: 2-4 hours/month Risk: None (funds pass through fiscal sponsor) Return: Credit as Steward. First-mover position. No equity.

# ---

### **Role 2: Door Opener**

# *"You know someone. You make one introduction. You say: 'Talk to Joseph.' That is the job. You do not need to understand ERES."*

# Time: 15 minutes Risk: None Return: Gratitude. Credit if desired.

# ---

### **Role 3: Legal / Publisher Liaison**

# *"You have a legal team or a publisher contact. You say: 'Review this one-page license.' That is the job. I do the rest."*

# Time: 1-2 hours Risk: None (review only, no signing unless agreed) Return: Credit. First access to the Trilogy.

# ---

### **Role 4: Pilot Implementer**

# *"You run the deployment. You manage the developers. You report to me on architecture needs. You do NOT handle money — the Steward does that."*

# Time: 10-20 hours/week during pilot (3-6 months) Risk: Technical (software deployment risk, not financial) Return: Co-ownership of pilot results. Published case study co-author. Credit.

# ---

### **Role 5: Developers (2)**

# *"You build what I specify. Semantic search. Simulation engine. Query interface. You follow the technical whitepaper. You do NOT design the architecture."*

# Time: 10-20 hours/week Compensation: Paid by Steward (hourly or contract) Return: Salary. Credit in technical documentation. Open-source contribution record.

# ---

### **Role 6: Civic Partner**

# *"You provide a sandbox. A municipality. A cooperative. A corporate internal environment. You say: 'Test ERES here.' You do NOT fund the entire pilot alone."*

# Time: Minimal (MOU signature, quarterly check-in) Risk: Reputational (if pilot fails) Return: First-mover positioning. Published case study co-author.

# ---

### **Role 7: Advisory Board Member**

# *"You attend four calls per year. You open your network. You say 'have you considered X?' You do NOT manage operations or handle money."*

# Time: 4-8 hours/year Risk: None Return: Credit. Strategic influence. First access.

## **IF YOU ARE THE MONEY HANDLER (STEWARD)**

You said: *"I handle money. Joseph never sees it."*

| Step | Action | Time |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| 1 | Choose a fiscal sponsorship vehicle (Open Collective, 501(c)(3), or corporate restricted fund) | 1-2 hours |
| 2 | Send Joseph: *"I have chosen \[vehicle\]. Send me your monthly number and first invoice."* | 5 minutes |
| 3 | Receive Joseph's number. Double it for safety. Set up monthly payment schedule. | 30 minutes |
| 4 | Pay bills as they arrive. Do not ask Joseph "how much does this cost." | 15 min/month |
| 5 | Once per quarter, send Joseph: *"Balance X. Spent Y. No action needed."* | 5 minutes |
| 6 | If you cannot continue, give 30 days notice. Name one potential successor. | 5 minutes |

You are done. That is the entire job.

---

## **IF YOU ARE THE DOOR OPENER**

You said: *"I know someone who should look at this."*

| Step | Action | Time |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| 1 | Identify one person in your network. Do not overthink. | 5 minutes |
| 2 | Send them: *"Talk to Joseph. He built something you should see. I cannot explain it. Just talk to him."* | 5 minutes |
| 3 | Copy Joseph on the message or let him know who to expect. | 2 minutes |
| 4 | You are done. No follow-up required unless you want to. | – |

That is it. One introduction. You do not need to understand ERES.

---

## **IF YOU ARE THE LEGAL / PUBLISHER LIAISON**

You said: *"I have a legal team or a publisher contact."*

| Step | Action | Time |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| 1 | Send Joseph: *"Here is the contact. Here is how to reach them. Use my name."* | 5 minutes |
| 2 | OR: Review the ERES Open Publication License (CCAL v2.1) yourself. Send Joseph any questions. | 1-2 hours |
| 3 | If publisher: Forward Joseph's Trilogy prospectus to your contact. Say: *"This is worth a look."* | 10 minutes |
| 4 | If legal: Confirm the license is usable. Say: *"No red flags."* OR *"Change X before signing."* | 1-2 hours |

You do not need to become Joseph's lawyer. You just need to open the door or give a quick review.
