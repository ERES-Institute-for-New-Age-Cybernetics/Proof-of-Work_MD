# **EXECUTIVE ORDER**

ESTABLISHING THE OFFICE OF FAMILY AMITY, THE PATRIOT DIVIDEND PROGRAM, AND THE NATIONAL CIVIC INFRASTRUCTURE

By the authority vested in me as President by the Constitution and the laws of the United States of America, including Article II, Section 1; Article II, Section 3; the National Emergencies Act (50 U.S.C. 1601 et seq.); the Economy Act of 1932 (31 U.S.C. 1535); and Title 5, United States Code, it is hereby ordered as follows:

This Order is issued in Youngstown Zone One (*Youngstown Sheet & Tube Co. v. Sawyer*, 343 U.S. 579 (1952)), acting with express or implied statutory authority from Congress. No provision purports to legislate, appropriate funds beyond existing authority, or create private rights of action. All permanent institutional structures require subsequent congressional action.

## **SECTION 1\. Findings.**

The American family is the foundational unit of civic life, yet no federal agency holds primary responsibility for family wellbeing as a systemic, measurable objective. Existing programs are fragmented across HHS, Education, HUD, VA, and other departments, creating duplication and service gaps.

The digital identity infrastructure of the United States remains vulnerable to synthetic identity fraud, deepfake spoofing, and quantum computing attacks. A quantum‑resistant, citizen‑sovereign architecture is now technically feasible.

A reciprocal economic contract between the government and the citizenry—direct relief during systemic stress combined with long‑term infrastructure investment—strengthens both immediate resilience and generational continuity.

---

## **SECTION 2\. Office of Family Amity.**

(a) There is established within the Executive Office of the President the Office of Family Amity.

(b) The Office shall coordinate all federal programs affecting family wellbeing, evaluate policies by the Hundred‑Year Question (*Does this strengthen the American family over the next century?*), and develop the National Family Wellbeing Index as a supplementary measure of national progress.

(c) The Office shall immediately establish the 24/7 Resonance Testing Hotline (1-800-RESONATE) , staffed by trained mediators and counselors, providing anonymous, no‑retribution support. “Resonance Testing” refers to structured conflict assessment and de‑escalation protocols—not medical diagnosis or unproven modalities.

(d) Within 90 days, the President shall submit to Congress proposed legislation to establish the Department of Family Amity as a Cabinet‑level department.

---

## **SECTION 3\. Patriot Dividend Program.**

(a) There is established the Patriot Dividend Program, a time‑bound direct economic transfer to verified American adults during periods of declared national economic emergency.

(b) Funding shall be drawn from:  
 (1) Windfall Recapture — a temporary surcharge on corporate profits exceeding 10% above the five‑year average in essential sectors (food, energy, housing, healthcare), as designated by the Secretary of Commerce;  
 (2) Patriot Bonds — Treasury‑issued, non‑marketable securities sold to American citizens and permanent residents, bearing interest at the 10‑year Treasury rate plus 50 basis points, redeemable after program termination;  
 (3) Voluntary Recirculation — as provided in subsection (c).

(c) Citizens who voluntarily return part or all of their Dividend shall receive Patriot Credits at a 1.25x multiplier, applicable toward tax liability, education, housing, or healthcare.

(d) Participants who opt into the credit system shall receive the GAIA Starter Pack — a personal digital node comprising verified identity, merit‑tracking ledger, and credential pathway. No citizen shall be penalized for non‑participation.

---

## **SECTION 4\. National Identity and Security Architecture.**

(a) The Director of the Office of Family Amity, in coordination with the Secretary of Homeland Security and the Director of NIST, shall develop and deploy the IDIPITIS four‑layer quantum‑resistant identity architecture as the national standard for programs established by this Order. The architecture shall be citizen‑sovereign: no access without explicit, revocable, informed consent.

(b) IDIPITIS shall incorporate the FAVORS six‑factor biometric verification system, including aura liveness detection, as the authentication standard. NIST shall publish a Federal Information Processing Standard (FIPS) within 180 days, drawing on available open‑source specifications.

(c) All algorithms and specifications shall be open‑source under a permissive license to permit independent security audit.

(a) There is established the Meritcoin‑Gracechain civic infrastructure as the economic and governance backbone for programs under this Order.

(b) Meritcoin shall operate on Proof‑of‑Resonance — value accrual through the four Bio‑Energetic Resonance Assessment metrics (Aura, Emission, Resonant Harmony Cycle, Resonant Continuity Index). It is not a security, commodity, or currency under existing federal law.

(d) The GAIA Starter Pack shall include immediate Standards Resonance Testing and a personalized SLA\_Ramp‑Up — a service‑level agreement accelerating citizen access to civic services.

## **SECTION 6\. Governance and Civic Participation.**

(a) All programs under this Order shall operate under the Citizen‑Business‑Government‑Military‑Ombudsmen‑Dignitaries‑Diplomats (CBGMODD) seven‑stakeholder governance standard. No single stakeholder category holds veto authority.

(b) Civic participation is expanded through the voluntary Electronic Civic Service (ECS) pathway — a modern, digital‑first complement to traditional service. No conscription is imposed.

(c) Voting, service, and contribution shall be transparently counted through open‑source infrastructure, subject to continuous stakeholder review.

## **SECTION 7\. Implementation and Funding.**

(a) Phase 1 (Days 1–90): Establish Office of Family Amity; launch 1‑800‑RESONATE Hotline; begin interagency coordination; initiate NIST specification process.

(b) Phase 2 (Days 91–180): Launch CBGMODD advisory councils in ten pilot districts (starting Northwest Arkansas); begin Patriot Dividend eligibility infrastructure.

(c) Phase 3 (Days 181–365): Deploy GAIA Starter Packs in pilot districts; issue first SCALULAR credentials; submit DOFA legislation to Congress.
