# **Key Discoveries & Formulations**

## **1\. The Version C Insight**

Without BERA, DOFA is a good idea. With BERA, it’s a different category of thing—it’s governance that can learn. Version C is the document that does not split the baby.

## **2\. The Consumption-to-Care Flip**

The hinge of the entire architecture. Consumption logic: extract, spend, deplete, repeat (system eats its own body). Care logic: invest, nurture, regenerate, compound (system feeds its own body). The DOFA closed loop is the mechanism that flips from one to the other. This is what creates the pool.

## **3\. The Infomediary as Institutional Care Logic**

The infomediary is the third thing between market (extraction) and government (capture risk). It stewards data and value on behalf of individuals as a fiduciary—IDIPITIS at the personal scale. This is what makes UBIMIA operationally possible.

## **4\. The Sustainability Reframe**

Sustainability is a blank check, not a constraint. Abundance follows from eliminating systemic waste. The 1000-Year Future Map asks "what could we do with a thousand years if we stopped wasting everything on preventable crises?"

## **5\. The 53-State Body**

Future-map vision: 50 US \+ Canada \+ Bahamas \+ Mexico integrated not by political merger but by shared protocol. Bahamas as financial-infomediary pilot node. Diplomat seat on CBGMODD is the architectural door that holds this open.
