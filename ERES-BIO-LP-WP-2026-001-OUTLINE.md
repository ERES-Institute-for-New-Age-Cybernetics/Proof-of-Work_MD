### L3 — THE PRIVATE NODE (Long-Term / FUTURE)
- Position: Opposite side of the disease body from the patient — the hidden equilibrium

- Key mechanism: The Pharmacy–Laboratory Bridge
  - Sea change: formalization moves from laboratory to pharmacy
  - Concentrated ingredients enabling on-site personalized compounding
  - Clinical trial infrastructure (robotic technology, ML/AI systems) as bridge scaffolding
- Partnership model: domain expertise (ERES) × clinical execution (trial-site capability) × investment vision

### L4 — THE WEANING EQUILIBRIUM (Stable Triangle Point)

  - Patient health trajectory recorded as immutable resonance record
  - Proof-of-Resonance (not Proof-of-Work): "It's not mining — it's tuning"
  - BERA metrics (ARI, ERI, RHC, RCI) feeding health-value accrual
- The purest form: lifestyle advantages for willing participants
- Meritcoin as health-improvement incentive — biological betterment earns value

### L5 — THE PLANETARY HEALTH EQUILIBRIUM (Stable Triangle Point)
