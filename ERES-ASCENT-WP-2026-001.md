# **2\. The Six-Step Pipeline**

The pipeline is sequential and contained: each step is a precondition for the next, and each later step operates only on outputs validated at the earlier steps. The headline structural finding is that Grace precedes Offer — Grace establishes the field that permits honest offering, and the offering arrives undegraded by fear-of-retribution because Grace has eliminated that tax in advance.

* Step 1 — Grace: field established (anti-retaliation, anonymity, amnesty, rehabilitation, safety net, faithful receipt).

* Step 2 — Offer: act enabled by Grace; candidate, sensor, signal, or testimony presents in true form.

* Step 3 — Reserve: faithful holding; offering is attested, timestamped, custody-chained, rendered into the Federated Reserve.

* Step 4 — Dictum: standing rule-set consults the Reserve under BEST/SOUND/GOOD.

* Step 5 — Deserve: Dictum × INPUT produces the Reserve/Deserve ratio.

* Step 6 — Disbursement: action proceeds proportionally; simulator output published to CCAL; response capability held in reserve.

# **3\. Four Rules for Ascension**

These govern upward path from Tier 1 SSSC toward any role of consequence. They cluster at the enabling end of the pipeline (Grace, Offer, Dictum, Deserve).

Rule A1 — Pass the Gate Continuously. Ascension is contingent on continuous passage of BEST/SOUND/GOOD at the tier currently held. A single failure freezes ascension until re-passed. Three consecutive failures at the same layer trigger Trifecta review.

Rule A2 — Earn the Path, Don't Skip It. EarnedPath governs sequence. CPM establishes the critical path through SCALULAR pillars; WBS decomposes the work; PERT acknowledges uncertainty. No tier may be skipped, no skip may be purchased. Time-in-role is the irreducible factor.

Rule A3 — Serve the One Master at Every Step. Every ascension step is tested against FEE\_MALE \== Semantic \=== Earth First. Competence without master-fidelity is disqualifying because it produces apocalyptic horsemen rather than transformed ones.

Rule A4 — Carry the Adversary. No candidate ascends alone. Each cohort must include a Needed Adversary in root-care function. Twin Messiah principle (two figures in tension generating the resonant constant) is operationalized at every tier transition.

# **4\. Four Rules for Chief Planetary Nation Defender**

These govern the apex SSPS role and presuppose the ascension rules. They cluster at the acting end of the pipeline (Reserve, Dictum, Deserve, Disbursement).

Rule D1 — Detect by Resonance Before Radiation. Primary instrument is FAVORS-into-CBGMODD with Aura-factor (BERA ARI) as leading indicator.
