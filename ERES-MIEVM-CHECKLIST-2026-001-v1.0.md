# **How to Use This Checklist**

This MIEVM Logistical Ratings Checklist is the canonical evaluation instrument for MIEVM Round 1 of ERES-SAVING-HUMANITY-2026-001 v1.0. It is provided to all six LLM validator instruments to ensure commensurable scoring. Each instrument scores each sub-criterion independently on a 1–10 scale using the Pass Threshold / Scoring Guide provided. The weighted scores are then aggregated into a domain subtotal and a final composite mean.

| Scoring Protocol | 1\. Read the sub-criterion label and scoring guide carefully.2. Locate the corresponding section in ERES-SAVING-HUMANITY-2026-001 v1.0.3. Score 1–10: 10 \= fully meets the pass threshold with no gaps detected; 7–9 \= meets threshold with minor gaps; 4–6 \= partially meets with significant gaps; 1–3 \= fails to meet threshold.4. Enter your score in the Score column.5. Use the Notes column for brief rationale flags only (max 5 words).6. Complete the Summary Table on the final page to compute your composite mean. |
| :---: | :---- |

| Weight Notation | Weights (×1, ×2, ×3) reflect the relative importance of each sub-criterion to the ERES framework's core claims. ×3 criteria are load-bearing: a score below 7 on any ×3 criterion is a binding constraint that must be noted in the instrument's final commentary. |
| :---: | :---- |

| Convergent Mean | The MIEVM convergent mean is computed as: sum all six instruments' composite means ÷ 6\. Target: ≥8.5. Instruments scoring below 7.5 on composite mean are outlier-flagged and their domain-level scores are reviewed for systematic interpretation differences before the mean is finalized. |
| :---: | :---- |

Important: Score only what is present in the document. Do not penalize for what is not claimed. Do not reward for what is claimed but not substantiated. If a sub-criterion is not addressed in the document, score it 1 and note 'Not addressed.'
